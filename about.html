Our Story

About Charm

We are building the world's most trusted marketplace for creative equipment — where creators discover, rent, and share the tools that bring their vision to life.

The Beginning

Born from a creative need

Charm started in 2023 in a small studio in East London, where two filmmakers realised something was broken. The best creative equipment was locked away in warehouses, gathering dust — while talented creators across the city couldn't afford access to the tools they needed.

We built Charm to fix that. A peer-to-peer marketplace where creators can list, discover, and rent premium equipment — cameras, lenses, lighting, audio gear, studio spaces — all with the trust and protection of a platform designed by creators, for creators.

Today, Charm connects over 12,000 creatives across 45+ countries, powering everything from indie shorts to commercial shoots. Every transaction is backed by our insurance programme, verified reviews, and a dedicated support team that understands the creative workflow.
