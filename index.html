Our Story
Born from a passion
for creative freedom

Charm was founded by a collective of musicians, photographers, and venue owners who shared a common frustration: finding the right creative space was unnecessarily hard. We built the platform we always wished existed.

Today, Charm is a full ecosystem — connecting creators with spaces, professionals, products, training, and events across 45+ countries. We believe creativity shouldn't be limited by access to space.
