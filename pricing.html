Frequently asked

The things most creators want to know before committing.

Can I switch plans at any time? +

Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan from your account settings at any time. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll only be charged the prorated difference. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

What exactly is the platform fee? +

The platform fee is a small percentage taken from each completed sale, in addition to standard payment processing fees. Free tier members pay 8%, Pro members 4%, and Studio members just 2%. This covers payment processing, fraud protection, buyer guarantees, and platform infrastructure.

Is there a free trial for Pro or Studio? +

Pro comes with a 14-day free trial — no card required. Studio plans include a personalised demo and onboarding session. Contact us and we'll set it up for you.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. For Studio plans, we also offer invoice-based billing and bank transfer. All transactions are securely processed via Stripe.

Still unsure? Talk to us.

Our team is happy to walk you through which plan fits your creative workflow best.
