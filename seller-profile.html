About

Professional cinematographer with over 12 years in the industry, working across feature films, commercial productions, and documentary projects. Based in London, I've had the privilege of collaborating with major studios and independent filmmakers alike. My kit is meticulously maintained, professionally serviced every 6 months, and always delivered clean. I understand what it means to need reliable gear on set — so I treat every rental as if it's my own production on the line.

I specialise in high-end cinema camera systems (ARRI, RED, Blackmagic) and a carefully curated selection of cinema-grade lenses. All items come with full accessories, pelican cases, and I offer optional operator services for select bookings.
